RED FLAGS

A concussion is a type of brain injury typically caused by a traumatic bump or blow to the head. Brain injuries are serious and can result in permanent damage if not taken care of properly.

If you are exibiting any of the following symptoms, seek medical attention immediately:

-Headaches that worsen

-Seizures

-Focal neurological signs

-Drowsy and/or cannot wake up

-Repeated vomiting

-Slurred speech

-Cannot recognize people or places

-Increasing confusion or irritability

-Weakness or numbness in arms/legs

-Neck pain

-Unusual behavioral change

-Change in state of consciousness

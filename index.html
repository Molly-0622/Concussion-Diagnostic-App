RED FLAGS

If you are exibiting any of the following symtoms, seek medical attention immediately:

-Headaches that worsen

-Seizures

-Focal neurological signs

-Drowsy and/or cannot wake up

-Repeated vomiting

-Slurred speech

-Cannot recognize people or places

-Increasing confusion or irritability

-Weakness or numbness in arms/legs

-Neck pain

-Unusual behavioral change

-Change in state of consciousness
